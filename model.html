Cindrella

Cinderella is a kind and gentle girl who faces many hardships after her mother passes away. Her stepmother and stepsisters treat her like a servant, making her do all the chores in the house. Even through all the unfair treatment, Cinderella remains hopeful and kind-hearted. Her life changes when a royal ball is announced, and with the help of her Fairy Godmother, she gets a chance to attend in a magical gown and glass slippers. At the ball, the prince is enchanted by her grace, but she must leave before midnight, leaving behind a single glass slipper. The prince searches the kingdom for the girl who fits the slipper, and when he finds Cinderella, her true beauty and goodness are finally recognized. She leaves behind her unhappy past and begins a new life filled with love and happiness.

Repunzel

Rapunzel is a young girl with incredibly long, golden hair that holds magical healing power. She is taken away as a baby and raised in a tall tower by a woman named Mother Gothel, who keeps her hidden from the world. Although Rapunzel dreams of exploring outside and seeing the floating lanterns that appear every year on her birthday, she is afraid to disobey. One day, a brave traveler named Flynn Rider discovers the tower and helps her escape into the outside world. Rapunzel finally experiences freedom, friendship and the truth about her real identity as the lost princess. Her courage and kindness help her overcome fear and begin a new life where she is reunited with her true family.

Frozen

Frozen tells the story of two royal sisters, Elsa and Anna, who care deeply for each other but are separated by a powerful secret. Elsa has magical ice powers that she struggles to control, and out of fear of hurting others, she distances herself from Anna and the world. When her powers are accidentally revealed, Elsa escapes to the mountains and unknowingly traps their kingdom in eternal winter. Anna sets out on a brave journey with friends she meets along the way to bring her sister back home. Through love, understanding and courage, the sisters learn that the strongest magic is the bond between them, and Elsa finally learns to embrace who she is without fear.
